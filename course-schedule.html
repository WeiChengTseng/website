CSC 2626: Imitation Learning for Robotics

This page contains an outline of the topics, content, and assignments for the semester. Note that this schedule will be updated as the semester progresses, with all changes documented here.

Week Date Topic Prepare Slides Notebooks Assignments Project
1 Mon, Sep 9 Imitation learning vs supervised learning 📖 🖥️ 📋 ✍️
Compounding errors and mitigating them via DAgger 📖 🖥️ 📋
How losses affect compounding errors 📖 🖥️ 📋
Querying experts only when necessary (optional) 📖 🖥️ 📋
2 Mon, Sep 16 Intro to optimal control 📖 🖥️ 📋
Intro to model-based reinforcement learning 📖 🖥️ 📋
Intro to model-free reinforcement learning 📖 🖥️ 📋
Monotonic improvement of the value function (optional) 📖 🖥️ 📋
Learning dynamics well only where it matters for the value function (optional) 📖 🖥️ 📋 📂
3 Mon, Sep 23 Offline / batch reinforcement learning 📖 🖥️ 📋 ✍️
Transitioning from offline to online RL 📖 🖥️ 📋
Imperfect demonstrations and improving upon them 📖 🖥️ 📋
4 Mon, Sep 30 Imitation learning combined with RL and planning 📖 🖥️ 📋
Making cost-to-go queries to experts 📖 🖥️ 📋
Expert iteration 📖 🖥️ 📋
Imitation can improve search and exploration strategies 📖 🖥️ 📋
Learning from experts that have privileged information 📖 🖥️ 📋 📂 ✍️
5 Mon, Oct 7 Dynamic movement primitives 📖 🖥️ 📋
Using optimal control solvers as experts 📖 🖥️ 📋
6 Mon, Oct 14 Thanksgiving Monday 📂
No in-person lecture or office hours on Monday
TA office hours are on
7 Mon, Oct 21 Imitation as program induction 📖 🖥️ 📋
Modular decomposition of demonstrations into skills 📖 🖥️ 📋
(Hierarchical) imitation of multi-goal tasks 📖 🖥️ 📋
Inferring grammars and planning domains 📖 🖥️ 📋
8 Mon, Oct 28 Fall Reading Week
No lecture
No office hours
9 Mon, Nov 4 Inverse reinforcement learning 📖 🖥️ 📋
Inferring rewards from preferences 📖 🖥️ 📋
Task specification and human-robot dialog 📖 🖥️ 📋
Value alignment 📖 🖥️ 📋
10 Mon, Nov 11 Shared autonomy 📖 🖥️ 📋
Imitation with a human in the loop 📖 🖥️ 📋
11 Mon, Nov 18 Imitation learning from videos 📖 🖥️ 📋
Causal confusion in imitation learning 📖 🖥️ 📋
12 Mon, Nov 25 Representation learning for imitation 📖 🖥️ 📋
Generalization and safety guarantees for imitation 📖 🖥️ 📋
13 Mon, Dec 2 Project presentations
14 Mon, Dec 9 Final project submission 📂
